Quienes Somos?

Welcome to Nano Tours del Perú, an innovative and dynamic Tours Agency founded in 1993 with the purpose to showing to the world’s eyes the historical, culture and natural richness of our country, so that visitors have an unforgettable experience with magical landscapes and dazzling archaeological remains, and they are not just mere spectators. Always looking for the enrichment of body and soul, where you can interact with our people, sharing their customs and cultural events inherited by centuries, varied cuisine, crafts, and traditional medicine, also appreciate the archeological monuments with the worldview of who erected them.
